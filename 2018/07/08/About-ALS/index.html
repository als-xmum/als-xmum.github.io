About ALS

There is a link between human life, the earth and the universe. It is an undeniable fact that a human’s body is made up of the same matters that forms the Earth. One-third of these matters have already existed during the formation of the universe. Birth refers to the formation of matters while death refers to the of these matters. Eventually, those matters will replenish and bring benefits to the earth once again but in another form.

The human mind is marvelous indeed. It is no exaggeration to say that the human brain is an extraordinary creation create by god himself. There’s no other brain in the world that can generate the same kind of consciousness associated with human ingenuity, with our ability to think and decide. Even though a person leaves for the heaven, the memories that they made with their loved one will never be forgotten. When someone you love becomes a memory, the memory becomes an everlasting treasure. Although life come to an end eventually, it is the most wonderful thing in universe.

“一个人的肉体是由和地球一样古老的物质组成的，其中的三分之一年龄甚至和宇宙相当。”

对于 ALS 的患者来说，与他们僵硬木讷的躯体相对的，是他们浩瀚星空一般的精神世界。

不论是活着——由错综复杂的神经网络带来的深邃思考，
还是死亡——由体内原子的特定组合打散重组的过程，
都是复杂精妙如星河的存在。

或者说，他们的存在本身就是来自宇宙的美好。
